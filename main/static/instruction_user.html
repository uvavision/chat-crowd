Overview

In this interactive drawing game you will collaborate with a painter to draw the layout of a given image. The layout of an image includes objects in the image and their locations, and is represented by a set of rectangles and corresponding labels (see an example below). In this task you will act as the instructor and you are going to issue a drawing instruction to the painter, or answer painter’s clarifying question about the previous instruction.

An image and its layout (red rectangles and labels)

Steps

Step 1, Login. First you need to fill in ID and username in the login page. Currently we are only running a internal test so feel free to use any ID and username. Click start to enter the conversation.

Step 2, Give Instructions, Answer Questions, or Finish the Conversation. After you login to the application you will see an image layout reference on the left and a interactive drawing history on the right. Based on the conversation history you can take one of the three actions.

2.1 Given a instruction. If the conversation history is empty or the latest message is a finished drawing, you need to compose a new instruction for the painter, by referring the image layout on the left[1]. Type your instruction in the textbox[2], and press Enter to submit it to the conversation. The painter will answer your instruction later so don’t wait for it. After you submitting your instruction the job is done, click the submit button [4] to submit the task.

2.2 Answer questions. If the latest message is a clarifying question asked by the painter, as demonstrated in the following image, you need to compose an answer for the question. Type your answer in the textbox, and press Enter to submit it to the conversation. The instructor will answer your question later so don’t wait for it. Your job is done and submit your task.

 

2.3 Finish Conversation. If you believe that last drawing has completed the layout of the given image, there is no need to give further instructions. In this case just click the “FINISH DRAWING & SUBMIT TASK” button. It will send a special “END” token to end the conversation, and your task will also be submitted.

Thank you!
