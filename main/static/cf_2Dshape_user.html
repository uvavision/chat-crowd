Overview
In this interactive drawing game, you will act as an instructor, who describes an image via a chat interface to a painter. The painter should be able to follow your instruction to redraw (partially) the image. If the drawing is not completed, other instructors will continue to provide more instructions. English only for text messages.
Note: Do NOT wait for the responses from the painters. Your contribution will be evaluated later for bonus.

Steps
  1. Login with your "Contributor ID" and your choice of username. Click START to proceed.
  2. If you do this task for the first time, there will be a simple quiz to prepare you for the task. Once you pass the quiz, you will enter the task.
  3. Please review the chat history (previous instructions and the latest painting) and determine what to do, such as
    • - Prove instructions on how to draw some of the objects with descriptions of the shape, color and location. (press Enter to submit messages).
    • - Answer the questions from the painter or help correct mistakes in the latest painting if necessary.
  4. Click
    next task
    to start next ask or
    submit
    or submit the task.
  5. IMPORTANT After you complete the task, please copy the confirmation code to the crowdflower main task.
We thank you for your contributions. We hope to invite you back for more tasks.
