Overview
In this interactive drawing game, you will act as a painter to draw the a layout of an image on a canvas based on the instructions in the chat history (especially the latest messages). The layout of an image includes objects and their locations. All objects are represented by a set of rectangles (proportional to the size of the corresponding objects) with labels (see Figure 1).
Note: Do NOT wait for the responses from the instructors. Your contribution will be evaluated later for bonus.

Steps
  1. Login with your "Contributor ID" and your choice of username. Click START to proceed.
  2. If you do this task for the first time, there will be a simple quiz to prepare you for the task. Once you pass the quiz, you will enter the task.
  3. Please review the chat history (previous instructions and the latest canvas) and determine what to do, such as
    • - Ask clarifying questions if the previous instruction is not clear (press Enter to submit messages).
