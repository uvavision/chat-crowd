Overview

In this interactive drawing game you will collaborate with an instructor to draw the layout of a given image. The layout of an image includes objects in the image and their locations, and is represented by a set of rectangles and corresponding labels (see an example below). In this task you will act as the painter and you need to complete a drawing based on the instruction from the instructor; you could also ask clarifying questions if the instruction is unclear.

 

An image and its layout (red rectangles and labels)

Steps

Step 1, Login. First you need to fill in ID and username in the login page. Currently we are only running a internal test so feel free to use any ID and username. Click start to enter the conversation.

Step 2, Draw on the Canvas. After you login to the application you will see a canvas for painting on the left and a conversation history on the right. The latest message is an instruction for you to complete the drawing of the layout.

2.1 (optional). If you think the instruction is unclear you can ask clarifying questions in the textbox. Just press Enter to submit your question; the instructor will answer your question later, so don’t wait for it. Continue to next step.

2.2. Now you start drawing the layout on the canvas. A rectangle could be created by first clicking and then dragging your mouse. After you release your mouse a textbox will appear under the rectangle. Now you fill in the object name in the textbox and press Enter to submit. We have limits for the object names so you need to choose a name from the auto-complete suggestions as you are typing the name. You can also cancel rectangles by moving your mouse inside the rectangle and click the close button that appears. See the following gifs for a drawing demonstration.

Step 3, Submit Your Drawing. Now you have completed the drawing, click “SUBMIT CANVAS”[2] to submit it to the conversation. Your job is done and click “SUBMIT”[5] to submit the task.

Thank you!
