Overview
In this interactive drawing game, you will act as a painter to draw on a canvas based on the instructions in the chat history (especially the latest messages). You will work other contributors and your contribution will be evaluated for bonus.

Steps
  1. Login with your "Contributor ID" and your choice of username. Click START to proceed.
  2. If you do this task for the first time, there will be a simple quiz to prepare you for the task. Once you pass the quiz, you will enter the task.
  3. Please review the chat history (previous instructions and the latest canvas) and determine what to do, such as
    • - Ask clarifying questions if the previous instruction is not clear (press Enter to submit messages).
    • - Draw on the canvas and click
      submit canvas
      to submit your drawing . (see how to draw)
  4. Click
    next task
    to start next ask or
    submit
    or submit the task.
  5. IMPORTANT After you complete the task, please copy the confirmation code to the crowdflower main task.
