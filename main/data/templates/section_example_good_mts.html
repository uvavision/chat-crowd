A Good Example
user: Hi, I am looking for rental apartment.
YOU: Please tell me your preferences on location. (Asking for preferences.)
user: So, preferably midtown Manhattan or Brooklyn Heights
YOU: I will look for apartments in Midtown Manhattan for now. (Telling the user what you are doing.)
user: OK.
YOU: What is your price range and how many bedrooms are you looking for? (Asking for additional preferences to narrow the search.)
user: one-bed, between $1500 and $2900. prefer big windows.
YOU: Sorry that we cannot search by "big windows". Now searching one-bedroom apartments for you between $1499 and $2999. (Telling the user what you can do.)
user: Oh OK thanks.
YOU: Over 200 apartments in over 90 buildings. Do you have other preferences like subway lines or number of baths?
user: An apartment near line 2 would be great.
YOU: Okay, I see 50 apartments that meet your criteria. I think this one would be a good fit for you: 1600 BROADWAY, #3917 (Midtown, Manhattan) $2800; studio; 1 s, 592 square feet. (Suggesting an apartment.)
user: Actually I think I might want something a little cheaper than that.
YOU: Okay, how about this one: 5 BRYANT PARK #2613 (Midtown, Manhattan) $2500; studio; 1 bath (Adjusting to new information.)
user: That looks great, thanks!
YOU: You're welcome. Bye!
