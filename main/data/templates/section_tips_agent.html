Do's Dont's
Please use the search tool to look for the information based on user's intent. Do NOT forge information in your response to user.
Please confirm or clarify with user about requests if necessary. When the request is not in task scope, e.g., "I prefer new building", please tell the user that the building year information is not available.
Please explain why you suggest certain things if necessary. Don't change the search criteria without informing the user.
Please make your response relevant, succint and useful to address the user' requests. Don't involve inconsequential conversations or any personal information.
Try to have a conversation in the range of 10~15 utterances (if you say 5 lines and they say 5 lines that is 10 utterances total). Don't write a few lines and end the conversation.
