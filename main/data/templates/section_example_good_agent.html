user: Hi, I am looking for rental apartment.
YOU: Hi! Please tell me your preferences on location. (Asking for preferences.)
user: So, preferably midtown Manhattan or Brooklyn Heights.
YOU: Good news. There are 20 apartments available in midtown Manhattan. (Use the search tool and share user the results.)
user: OK.
YOU: What is your price range and how many bedrooms are you looking for? (Asking for additional preferences to narrow down the search.)
user: one-bed, between $1500 and $2900. prefer big windows.
YOU: Sorry that we cannot search by "big windows". Now searching one-bedroom apartments for you between $1499 and $2999. (Telling the user what you can do.)

user: That looks great, thanks!
