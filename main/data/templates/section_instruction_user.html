• There is a simple quiz for the first-time participants. It is mainly for preparing you for the task. After you login (or pass the quiz), you will see the chat interface.
  1. Begin your task by typing in [1] and press enter to submit your message.(Please note there is a brief pause between typing by design.)
  2. IMPORTANT When you specify your initial preferences on the rental apartments, please use the criteria [2] (for quality-control purpose). You can adjust them through the conversation.
  3. You may consider ending the conversation, if
    1. you have achieved your goal - possible options found.
    2. you have explored your options with the agent and the agent is not able to find an apartment meeting your criteria after 15+ utterances.
  4. IMPORTANT Click [3] and obtain the confirmation code to complete the task via CrowdFlower interface. Otherwise, we cannot reward you.
